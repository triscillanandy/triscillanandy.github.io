About Us

Yana Stream is a technology company focused on providing smart irrigation systems for farmers in remote areas. It aims to promote sustainable and efficient water management in agriculture using Long Range-based technology to collect data and monitor the water supply in real-time. The system will make informed decisions about watering crops and optimizing yields without farmer intervention. The company's vision is to revolutionize agriculture by providing innovative and cost-effective solutions for efficient water management and optimal crop growth.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to provide farmers in remote areas with an innovative, cost-effective solution for optimal crop growth and resource preservation. By leveraging the power of long-range, low-power communication, we strive to enhance the sustainability of agriculture in remote areas, enabling farmers to achieve better yields, reduce water waste, and preserve natural resources.

Objectives

  1. To analyze the strengths and weaknesses of current smart irrigation monitoring systems with a focus on Long Range technology.
  2. To design the architecture for a Long Range -based smart irrigation monitoring system that can collect and transmit real-time data on soil moisture levels, weather patterns, and other relevant environmental factors
  3. To develop a Long Range-based smart irrigation system using IoT-based devices and cloud computing that can optimize water usage and minimize waste in agriculture
  4. To test and validate the developed Long Range-based smart irrigation system in real-world agricultural settings, ensuring that it is accurate, efficient, and cost-effective.
  5. To continuously improve the Long Range-based smart irrigation system based on feedback from farmers, researchers, and other stakeholders, with the goal of creating a sustainable and innovative solution for efficient water management in agriculture.
